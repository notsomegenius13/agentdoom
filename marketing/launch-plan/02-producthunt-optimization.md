## Comment Response Templates

**"How is this different from v0/Bolt/Lovable?"**
> Great question. Those are developer tools — you get a code project. AgentDoom is a consumer platform — you get a live, deployed tool with a URL and it goes in a social feed. The output isn't code, it's a product that others can find, use, and remix. Think: Canva vs Photoshop.

**"What if AI models get commoditized?"**
> That's actually our thesis! Generation quality improves automatically as models improve. Our moat is the distribution layer — the feed, remix chains, social graph, and eventual marketplace. We're building the platform, not the model.

**"What can't it build?"**
> Anything multi-page, anything requiring external API integrations, or complex data persistence. It's purpose-built for single-screen utility tools — calculators, trackers, forms, generators. We intentionally constrain scope to keep the success rate at ~95%.

**"What's the business model?"**
> Three revenue streams: (1) Marketplace — creators sell tools, we take 15-20%, (2) Pro subscription at $14/mo — custom domains, analytics, no branding, (3) Boost — creators pay for feed placement. Free tier is generous to drive network effects.
