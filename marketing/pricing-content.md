## FAQ Section

### How does the free plan work?

You can create up to 10 tools per month, remix unlimited existing tools, and deploy them all to shareable URLs. Your tools will include a small "Made with AgentDoom" badge. No credit card required.

### What happens when I hit the free tier limit?

Your existing tools stay live and functional. You just can't generate new ones until the next month — or you upgrade to Pro for unlimited generation.

### Can I sell tools on the free plan?

Yes. Any creator can list tools for sale on the marketplace. Free users pay a 20% platform fee; Pro users pay a reduced 15% fee. You set the price ($1-50 per tool), and we handle payments via Stripe.

### What does "custom domains" mean?

Pro users can connect their own domain (e.g., tools.yourbrand.com) so your tools live at your URL instead of agentdoom.ai/t/your-tool. We handle SSL and hosting.

### Is there a free trial for Pro?

Yes — 7 days, full access, no credit card required. Cancel anytime before the trial ends and you won't be charged.

### Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. Upgrades are prorated. When you downgrade, you keep Pro features through the end of your billing period.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and Apple Pay via Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay by invoice.

### Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Annual billing is $120/year ($10/mo effective) — save 29% compared to monthly.
