## Launch Pricing Strategy Recommendations

### 1. Founding Member Discount: YES

**Offer: $9/mo forever (locked price) during launch week.**

- 36% discount drives urgency and conversion
- "Forever" lock creates FOMO and loyalty
- Cap at 1,000 founding members to create scarcity
- These users become evangelists — LTV far exceeds discount cost
- At $9/mo with 8% churn, average LTV = $112.50 vs $175 at $14/mo — still profitable

### 2. Free Trial: 7 days, no credit card

- Lower friction = more trial starts
- 7 days is enough to generate 3-5 tools and see value
- No-card trials convert at 5-8% vs 15-20% with card, but generate 3-5x more trials
- Net effect: more Pro users from no-card approach at this stage

### 3. Annual vs Monthly

- Launch with monthly only for first 30 days (reduces decision friction)
- Introduce annual at $120/yr ($10/mo) in month 2
- 29% savings vs monthly — strong incentive
- Target: 20% annual adoption by month 3, reduces churn significantly

### 4. Marketplace Fees vs Subscription Timing

- Launch with subscriptions (Pro tier) on day 1 — simpler, predictable MRR
- Marketplace transactions launch in Phase 3 (month 2-3) per master spec
- Start at 20% platform fee for free users, 15% for Pro
- Do NOT reduce fees further — 15% is already competitive (Gumroad: 10%, App Store: 30%)
- Marketplace revenue will eventually surpass subscription revenue (optimistic: month 4-5)
